PART 1; Making a new account

So this is where you start off, new account to make. But how? You need to make a new name, and also an email. Email is pretty important for this step.

If you don't want to use your personal email, you have to use a temporary mail. There's often chances that it won't work and spit out this error:

But if you manage to get the email disposed to you and create the account.. You're good!
